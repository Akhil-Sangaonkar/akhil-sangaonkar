About Me

Data Scientist | ML Engineer | Photographer

My journey began with a deep-seated love for mathematics, a subject that always fascinated me with its elegance and logical structure. This passion fueled my ambition and led me to one of the most prestigious competitive institutes in India, where I earned a remarkable 99% score to secure admission at IIT Madras for Aerospace Engineering. The rigorous curriculum not only honed my analytical skills but also introduced me to the intricacies of complex systems and problem-solving in a highly technical field.

While immersed in my studies, I discovered a creative outlet in photography. What started as a simple hobby soon evolved into a profound passion, particularly for landscape photography. Capturing the beauty of nature became my way of expressing creativity and appreciating the world around me. The interplay of colors, light, and shadows in a landscape can tell a story that numbers alone cannot convey. This realization sparked a new dimension in my life, merging my technical and creative pursuits.

As I explored the vast realm of photography, I began to draw parallels between the artistic and the analytical. This connection illuminated my path toward data science, a field that perfectly blends my interests. I realized that machine learning, with its mathematical foundations, is a natural extension of my love for mathematics. Data science allows me to apply mathematical principles to derive insights and solve real-world problems, much like how I analyze a scene to create a compelling photograph.

I invite you to join me on this journey as I continue to explore the intersections of data, creativity, and storytelling.
